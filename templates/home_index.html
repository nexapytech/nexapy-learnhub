Nexapy LearnHub Community Guidelines and Code of Conduct

The Nexapy LearnHub thrives on the strength of our community. Therefore, it is crucial that we nurture a space that is open, warm, and inviting.

Members of Nexapy LearnHub:

  • Show Consideration: We are mindful of our fellow members
  • Demonstrate Respect: We honor others and their positions, skills, commitments, and efforts
  • Display Empathy: We listen attentively and handle differing opinions with tact
  • Embrace Constructive Feedback: When we disagree, we voice our concerns respectfully
  • Use Inclusive Language: We welcome everyone to participate, creating an environment where all voices matter
  • Have Fun: If we’re not enjoying ourselves and each other’s company, then we’re missing the mark!

As members of Nexapy LearnHub, we pledge to uphold the standards and principles outlined in the Python Software Foundation’s Code of Conduct. Explore our Community Slack Guide for tips on effective questioning, clear communication, and improving the readability of your code examples.
